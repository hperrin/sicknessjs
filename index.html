Sickness.js

Sickness is a JavaScript library that aims to emulate what it's like after an entire day of coding, when you're coming down off your Red Bull high, and everything starts to look a little jittery.

It's named Sickness because if you experience this feeling, you have a sickness, just like me. It's not a bad thing. It's a productive sickness. Workaholics, perfectionists, enthusiasts; good names for the same people. It makes your boss happy, but your partner might not feel the same. Maybe this page will remind you to take a break. You can always come back to it tomorrow.

Good luck and happy coding.
